Application Workflow

Every teacher registration application follows this lifecycle.

Draft Submitted Under Review Ready for Approval Approved
Rejected at any review stage
Draft Application started, not yet submitted
Submitted Sent to MOE for processing
Under Review Reviewer is checking documents
Ready Awaiting final decision by TR committee
Approved Accepted by TR committee, teacher registered
Rejected Declined by reviewer or TR committee

Registration Statuses

Once approved, each teacher holds one of these registration levels.

Full Fully qualified — no conditions or restrictions
Full Conditional Conditions must be met to maintain status
Provisional Temporary while completing qualifications
Limited Restricted scope or duration limits
Expired Registration lapsed — renewal required
